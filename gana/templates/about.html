About Us

Welcome to Avanthi Engineering College, located in the beautiful town of Garividi in Andhra Pradesh. Established in the year 2001, our college has been a beacon of excellence in engineering education. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation and creativity in our students.

Our Location

Avanthi Engineering College is situated in the serene surroundings of Garividi, Andhra Pradesh. Our state-of-the-art campus offers a conducive environment for learning and personal growth.

Courses Offered

At Avanthi Engineering College, we offer a variety of undergraduate engineering programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required for a successful career in the industry.

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

Computer Science and Engineering - AI & ML (CSM)

Mechanical Engineering (MECH)

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Why Choose Us?

With a legacy of over two decades, Avanthi Engineering College stands out for its dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and focus on practical learning. We aim to create a transformative impact on our students and prepare them for the challenges of the 21st century.
